The pipeline

What happens after you hit generate

01

Script locked

Your pasted script (or a written-for-you one) is split into 5-second beats matched to your chosen length.

02

Voice laid down

Every beat is voiced in the language and tone you picked — timed precisely to the script.

03

Visuals built

Each beat gets a matching clip — avatar, stock footage, or an image brought to life — based on your style choice.

04

Edited & rendered

Captions, music, and transitions are applied automatically in your chosen frame size. No editor needed.

Where the visuals come from

Three sources, one stitched video

Avatar

AI presenter on camera

A lip-synced presenter delivers your script to camera. Best for trust-driven niches like finance or education.

Stock

Footage matched to each line

Every 5-second beat pulls footage that fits the line, narrated over the top. No face required.

Image-to-video

Your own images, brought to life

Upload photos or graphics — each one is animated with subtle motion and looped to fill its 5-second beat.

Editing, built in

Trim & reorderDrag any 5-second beat earlier, later, or out entirely — the voice re-times itself automatically.

CaptionsAuto-generated captions, editable inline, in the same language as the voiceover.
