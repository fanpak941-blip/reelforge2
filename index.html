FAQ
Frequently Asked Questions
Do I need video editing experience to use ReelForge?+
Not at all. ReelForge is designed for complete beginners. Simply paste your script or topic, choose your settings, and the platform handles everything — voiceover, visuals, editing, captions, music, and thumbnails. No technical skills required.
How long does it take to generate a video?+
Average render time is approximately 4 minutes for a 5-minute video. Longer videos take proportionally more time. A 30-minute video typically renders in 20–25 minutes. You'll see a live progress tracker during generation.
Are the videos copyright-free? Can I monetize them on YouTube?+
Yes. All stock footage is licensed for commercial use, the AI voiceover is fully owned by you, and background music is royalty-free for YouTube monetization. You own 100% of the videos you create with ReelForge.
What is the AI Avatar feature?+
The AI Avatar feature places a realistic, lip-synced digital presenter in your video who delivers your script to camera — just like a real YouTube host. This is unlocked in the Professional plan and above.
Can I edit the video after it's generated?+
Yes. ReelForge includes a built-in browser-based editor. You can trim or reorder 5-second clips, swap visuals, edit captions, change the voice, adjust music volume, and re-export in any frame size — without leaving the platform.
How does the YouTube SEO package work?+
After your video is generated, ReelForge automatically creates an SEO-optimized YouTube title, full description with timestamps, 30 niche-specific tags, and a viral keyword analysis for your content category. Simply copy-paste this into YouTube Studio when uploading.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime?+
Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time with no penalties or fees. Your access continues until the end of your current billing period. We believe in earning your subscription every month, not locking you in.
Is there an API available for developers and enterprises?+
Yes. ReelForge Enterprise includes full API access for custom integrations, automated workflows, and white-label implementations. Contact our enterprise sales team for documentation and pricing.
