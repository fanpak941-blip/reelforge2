No. ReelForge is designed for complete beginners. You only need to provide a script or topic — the platform handles everything else automatically.

On average, a 5-minute video renders in approximately 4 minutes. Longer videos take proportionally more time. You'll see a live progress bar throughout the process.

Yes. All videos created with ReelForge use royalty-free music and stock footage. Your generated content is yours to publish and monetize freely.

Absolutely. There are no long-term contracts or cancellation fees. Cancel anytime directly from your account dashboard.
