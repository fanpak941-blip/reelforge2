Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 13, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly when creating an account, such as your name, email address, and payment information. We also collect usage data, including videos created, scripts entered, and feature interactions.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the ReelForge platform
  • To process payments and manage subscriptions
  • To send service updates and product announcements
  • To provide customer support

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely on encrypted servers. Generated videos are retained for 30 days, after which they are automatically deleted. We never sell your personal data to third parties.

4. Cookies

We use essential cookies to operate the platform and analytics cookies to improve our service. You may disable non-essential cookies in your browser settings.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. Contact us at privacy@reelforge.app for any data-related requests.

6. Contact

For privacy concerns, email us at privacy@reelforge.app or use the contact form on our website.
